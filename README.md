## Business Outcome Translator

A lightweight tool that helps Customer Success teams translate product capabilities into clear business outcomes and executive-ready messaging.

Bridges the gap between technical capabilities and business value.

---

## What It Produces

- Operational outcomes  
- Business value statements  
- Executive narratives  
- QBR talking points

## How It Works

Input:
- Customer goal  
- Customer challenge  
- Product capability  
- Audience (e.g., Executive, Technical)  

Output:
- Clear, structured value messaging  

---

## Example Use

**Input:**  
Improve visibility into unmanaged devices  

**Output:**  
- Outcome: Continuous asset visibility  
- Value: Reduced risk from unknown assets  
- Narrative: Clear executive-level explanation  
- QBR: Talking point for stakeholder alignment
